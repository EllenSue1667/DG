Lesson 304

Mechanics - Capitalization

Capitalize proper nounsProper nouns name a special person, place, or thing and begin with capital letters.
Source: Lesson 18.  A proper noun names a particular person, place, or thing.

Examples:
girl - Rebecca
city - New Orleans
newspaper - New York Times

 
 
 

Instructions: Capitalize each word that needs a capital letter.

1. Should you and i go with sam?

Should you and I go with Sam?

2. The magna carta is an important document.

The Magna Carta is an important document.

3. the first war in my lifetime was world war II.

The first war in my lifetime was World War II.

4. I live in lindon, utah.

I live in Lindon, Utah.

5. Have you and john visited england?

Have you and John visited England?
